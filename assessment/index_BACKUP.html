Assessment Guidelines

🔒
Confidential & Private
Your assessment is completely confidential. All information is stored securely and will only be used to provide personalized recommendations.
✓
No Right or Wrong Answers
There are no correct or incorrect answers. Be honest about your experiences and challenges. Your responses help us understand your unique situation better.
❓
Choose "Not Sure" If Confused
If you're unsure about any question or option, feel free to select "Not Sure". This helps us provide you with broader recommendations.
🎯
Personalized Results
Your results are tailored specifically to your profile, interests, and abilities. Each recommendation is designed to match your unique needs.
⏱️
Takes Approximately 15-20 Minutes
The assessment typically takes 15-20 minutes to complete. You can take breaks and return to complete it at your own pace.
♿
Accessible for All Users
The assessment is designed to be accessible for users with various disabilities. Screen readers, keyboard navigation, and other assistive technologies are fully supported.
