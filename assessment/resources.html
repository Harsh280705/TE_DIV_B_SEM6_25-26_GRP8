Essential guides, tools, and support for persons with disabilities

🏛️ Popular Central Government Schemes

Essential government schemes designed to support persons with disabilities across education, employment, and financial assistance.

1️⃣ UDID Card (Unique Disability Identity Card)

What is it? An official government identity card issued by the Department of Empowerment of Persons with Disabilities (DEPwD) that provides one unique identity number to every person with disability in India.

Why is it Important?

  • Access education benefits (scholarships, fee concessions, reservations)
  • Get job reservations and attend special recruitment drives
  • Apply for government loans, pensions, and subsidies
  • Receive healthcare benefits and assistive devices
  • Valid nationwide - avoid carrying multiple documents

Who Can Apply?

Any person who has a disability recognized under the RPwD Act, 2016 can apply. The 21 recognized disabilities include:

Acid Attack Victim, Autism Spectrum Disorder, Blindness, Cerebral Palsy, Chronic Neurological Conditions, Haemophilia, Hearing Impairment, Intellectual Disability, Leprosy-cured persons, Locomotor Disability, Low Vision, Mental Illness, Multiple Disabilities, Multiple Sclerosis, Muscular Dystrophy, Parkinson's Disease, Dwarfism, Sickle Cell Disease, Specific Learning Disability, Speech & Language Disability, Thalassemia

Application Process

  1. Visit: Apply at Swavlamban Portal
  2. Choose application method (Aadhaar or Aadhaar enrollment number)
  3. Fill the online form with personal, disability, and employment details
  4. Upload required documents (Aadhaar, address proof, photo, disability certificate, income certificate)
  5. Submit for medical verification
  6. Timeline: Around 9 days for processing

After You Apply

  • Application sent to CMO / DMO (Medical Authority)
  • Specialist doctor assesses the disability
  • Medical Board decides type, percentage, and validity
  • UDID number and disability certificate generated
  • Card printed and dispatched or made available online

Download Your e-UDID Card

Visit Swavlamban Portal and enter your enrollment number, date of birth, and captcha to download your e-UDID Card (PDF) which is valid for official use.

Track Your Application

Visit the portal and click "Track Your Application" to enter your enrollment number, Aadhaar number, mobile number, or UDID number to see real-time status.

Renewal & Card Types

  • Temporary Cards: Can be renewed up to 9 months before expiry
  • Permanent Cards: Valid for life (especially for persons above 18 with permanent disability)
  • By Disability Percentage: White Card (below 40%), Yellow Card (40–80%), Blue Card (above 80%)

Benefits of UDID Card

  • Education: Scholarships, fee concessions, reserved seats
  • Employment: Government job reservations, special recruitment drives, skill development
  • Financial Support: Loans, subsidies, disability pensions
  • Healthcare: Free / discounted treatment, assistive devices, priority services
  • Convenience: One card instead of multiple documents, valid all over India, online access

2️⃣ ADIP Scheme (Assistance to Disabled Persons)

What is it? A Government of India welfare scheme that provides free or highly subsidized assistive devices to persons with disabilities so they can move independently, communicate better, and live with dignity.

What Kind of Help Does ADIP Provide?

  • Mobility Aids: Wheelchairs (manual/motorized), tricycles, crutches, walkers, prosthetic limbs
  • Visual Impairment: Braille kits, white canes, talking watches, low-vision devices
  • Hearing Impairment: Hearing aids, cochlear implant support
  • Intellectual & Multiple Disabilities: Special learning aids, therapy tools, cognitive development devices
  • Modern Assistive Technology: Smart devices, advanced prosthetics, digital assistive tools

Who Can Apply?

You must meet ALL the following conditions:

  • Be a Person with Disability (PwD) under RPwD Act, 2016
  • Have a valid Disability Certificate or UDID Card
  • Disability should be 40% or more
  • Monthly income: Up to ₹22,500/month → Free aids | Above limit → Subsidized support

How Much Financial Help?

  • Assistance provided in kind (devices), not cash
  • Maximum assistance value: Up to ₹55,000 for most aids
  • Higher limits for specific cases (like cochlear implants)
  • No need to buy device first - government provides it through approved agencies

How to Apply (Step-by-Step)

  1. Prepare Documents: UDID card/Disability Certificate, Aadhaar, income certificate, address proof, passport-size photo
  2. Find ADIP Agency: Contact government hospitals, District Social Welfare Offices, or authorized NGOs
  3. Medical Assessment: A medical authority assesses your disability and required aid
  4. Approval & Distribution: Once approved, device is ordered/fitted and provided free or subsidized

Replacement Schedule

  • Children: After 3 years
  • Adults: After 5 years
  • Earlier Replacement: If device is damaged or disability condition changes

Why ADIP is Important

  • Education: Enables access to learning tools and educational resources
  • Employment: Makes careers accessible with mobility and assistive aids
  • Independence: Reduces dependency on others
  • Skill Training: Makes vocational work possible
