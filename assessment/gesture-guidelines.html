Learn how to control the assessment using simple hand gestures

🎯 How It Works

  • No keyboard or mouse needed - Control everything with your hands
  • Your webcam will detect your hand gestures in real-time
  • Each gesture performs a specific action during the assessment
  • Questions will be read aloud automatically so you can focus on gestures
  • You can repeat any question by using the Repeat gesture

Supported Gestures

👍
YES
Thumbs Up
Selects "Yes" as your answer to the current question.
👎
NO
Thumbs Down
Selects "No" as your answer to the current question.
👉
SKIP
Point Forward
Skips the current question and moves to the next one.
🔁
REPEAT
Circular Motion
Repeats the current question aloud so you can hear it again.
✋
IDLE
Rest Position
No action is taken. Use this when you're thinking or not ready.

💡 Tips for Best Results

  • Good lighting: Make sure your hand is clearly visible
  • Keep hand steady: Hold each gesture for 1-2 seconds
  • Face the camera: Position your hand in front of the webcam
  • One gesture at a time: Wait between gestures to avoid confusion
  • Relax: The system is designed to be forgiving and accessible
